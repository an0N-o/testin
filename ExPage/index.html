We Are FnF

Farms and Farmers Foundation (FnF) is an award-winning non-government, non-profit organisation. It works with farmers, especially small holders, with an aim to improve their livelihood by increasing income from agricultural activities. Started in 2010 by young individuals from permier educational institutions, the FnF team is now comprised of highly qualified and experienced professionals from agricultural technology, agribusiness and social impact.
