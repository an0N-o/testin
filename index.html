We Are FnF

Farms and Farmers Foundation (FnF) is an award-winning non-government, non-profit organisation. It works with farmers, especially small holders, with an aim to improve their livelihood by increasing income from agricultural activities. Started in 2010 by young individuals from permier educational institutions, the FnF team is now comprised of highly qualified and experienced professionals from agricultural technology, agribusiness and social impact.

What We Do

We’ve got everything you need to launch and grow your business

DeHaat™ Center

DeHaat is easily accessible and single contact point for farmers for latest technology, source of quality inputs at affordable price, market linkage, and relevant information like market, existing government schemes, and crop schedule reminders.

Value Chain Support

FnF facilitates market linkage of best quality inputs (seeds, fertilizers, and crop protection), factors of production (credit, insurance, mechanization), and agri-produce (cereals, pulses, and F&V) so that farmers could maximize productivity and returns from their crop

DeHaat™ for Business

M-commerce platform for agri input and farm equipment
Farm based advisory on real time basis

DeHaat™ for Farmers

Farmers’ profile management
Exhaustive crop pest database in local language

Micro-entrepreneur Support

FnF identifies young and enthusiastic individuals with passion for agribusiness, and supports them to offer last mile agri services to farmers through their own center (called “DeHaat”). FnF hones their entrepreneurship skills by training, monitoring, logistical support and linking with markets.

FPO Support

FnF supports small and marginal farmers in collectivizing them into self-help groups (SHGs), producer groups (PGs), and farmer producer organizations (FPOs). In its role of a facilitator, FnF guides these groups in capacity building, leadership training, business planning, registration, financial linkage, and access to market.
